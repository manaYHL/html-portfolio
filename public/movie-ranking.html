The Best Movies According to Mana

My top 3 movies of all-time

Everything Everywhere All At Once

It's a story of an Asian immgration family in the U.S. It has a lot of twists and combines various genres like martial arts, scientific fictions, and family ethics. It has won the best picture in the Oscar Awards in 2023.

Portrait Of A Lady On Fire

This French movie is about two women falling in love with each other in the 19th centry. It's beautifully shot, and every scene in the movie is like a painting. It's one of the most beautiful romance movies in my opinion.

Harry Porter Series

Harry Porter Series are my childhood memories. They have an irreplaceable position in my mind. I like them not just because the story is fantastic, it's also because the performances of every actor and actress in the movie are extraordinary.
